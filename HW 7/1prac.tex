\section*{Solution 2: }
The recurrence relation can be given by 
\begin{align*}
u & = \sum_{k=0}^{n-1} a_k\ t^{k} \\
u'' & = \sum_{k=2}^{n-1} k (k-1)\ a_k\ t^{k-2}\implies \sum_{m=0}^{n-3}\ (m+2)(m+1)\ a_{m+2}\ t^m
\end{align*}
